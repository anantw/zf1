Ingredient Science
7,000 mg per serving
L-Arginine

Supports nitric oxide production and vasodilation, improving nutrient delivery to healing tissues. Critical substrate for collagen synthesis pathways and polyamine production essential for cell proliferation during wound closure.

7,000 mg per serving
L-Glutamine

Primary fuel for rapidly dividing immune cells and enterocytes. Supports intestinal mucosal integrity during metabolic stress and maintains gastrointestinal barrier function critical to systemic immune response in recovery.

2,500 mg per serving
Marine Collagen Peptides

Provides the critical hydroxyproline and proline amino acid profile for Type I and III collagen matrix formation. Cold-water marine source for superior molecular weight distribution and absorption across the gastrointestinal tract.

1,500 mg per serving
Ca-HMB (Calcium β-Hydroxy β-Methylbutyrate)

Attenuates muscle protein catabolism during catabolic states associated with surgical recovery and critical illness. Supports anabolic signalling through mTOR pathway activation, preserving lean mass while wound repair proceeds.
